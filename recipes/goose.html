Goose Recipe

Back

Description:

Maybe save this one for a dinner party. But the good news is that if you find it on the road, it’s still totally usable, don’t let anyone tell you otherwise. That’s good meat, no matter where it comes from. Just wash it off. You’ll need a good tangy recipe to get that gamey flavor out.

Ingredients:

  • 1 dead goose (can be roadkill)
  • 1 orange juiced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Orange zest

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Rinse the goose in cold water, and place in baking dish. Splash with vermouth, then season with salt; set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine orange zest, orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, garlic and soy sauce. Season with mustard and caraway. Pour glaze over goose in baking dish.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until no longer pink and juices run clear. Baste at least twice during baking.
  5. Serve
